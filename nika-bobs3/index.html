Tribute to Khvicha Kvaratskhelia

Khvicha Kvaratskhelia showcasing his skills on the field.

Khvicha Kvaratskhelia, born on February 12, 2001, in Tbilisi, Georgia, is a football prodigy who has quickly risen to prominence as one of Europe's most exciting young talents. Known for his exceptional dribbling, creativity, and vision, Kvaratskhelia has made a mark as a dynamic winger for both Napoli and the Georgian national team.

Growing up in Georgia, Khvicha was introduced to football at an early age, with his father, a former footballer, being a significant influence. He began his youth career at the Georgian club Dinamo Tbilisi, where his skills quickly caught the attention of scouts from top European clubs. He made his professional debut for Dinamo Tbilisi at just 16, showing early signs of the talent that would later define his career.

In 2019, at the age of 18, Khvicha moved to the Russian Premier League, joining Rubin Kazan. His performances in Russia helped him become one of the standout players in the league, drawing further attention from top clubs across Europe. It was in Russia that he earned the nickname "Kvara" for his style of play, which reminded many of some of the world's best wingers. Khvicha’s dazzling dribbles, pace, and ability to score and assist made him a fan favorite and an opponent’s nightmare.

In 2022, Khvicha made a big move to Italy’s Serie A, joining Napoli. His debut season in Italy was nothing short of spectacular, with his skillset becoming one of the main reasons for Napoli's success. He formed a potent attacking trio with Victor Osimhen and Giovanni Di Lorenzo, helping Napoli secure their first Serie A title in over 30 years during the 2022-2023 season. His performances in Serie A and the UEFA Champions League have firmly established him as one of the top talents in European football.

Trophies and Achievements

  • Serie A Champion - 2022-2023 with Napoli
  • Serie A Player of the Month - November 2022
  • Georgian Footballer of the Year - 2022
  • Top Scorer - Rubin Kazan (2020-2021 season)
  • Russian Cup Winner - 2020-2021 with Rubin Kazan
  • UEFA Champions League Group Stage - with Napoli (2022-2023)
  • Numerous Goals (71) and Assists (43) - Throughout his career in both domestic and international competitions
  • French League 1 Mcdonalds Champion - with Paris-Saint-German (2024-2025)

Despite his young age, Khvicha has already made significant strides in his career. He is widely regarded as one of the best young wingers in Europe, with his skillset being compared to some of the greatest players in the sport. His performances for the Georgian national team have also been remarkable, as he leads his country in major international competitions like the UEFA, Euro qualifiers, and World Cup qualifiers.

Seasons Overview

Khvicha's professional career has been full of exciting moments, and here’s a brief look at his seasons:

  • 2017-2018: Begins his professional career with Dinamo Tbilisi, making his debut at just 16 years old.
  • 2019-2020: Moves to Rubin Kazan, making an immediate impact with impressive performances in the Russian Premier League.
  • 2020-2021: Becomes one of the top players in the Russian Premier League, finishing as the top scorer for Rubin Kazan and winning the Russian Cup.
  • 2022-Present: Joins Napoli, quickly establishing himself as one of the most exciting players in Serie A. Leads Napoli to the Serie A title in the 2022-2023 season, a historic victory for the club. Now he transfered to PSG and already won the French league Championship adding another team trophy to his cabinet.

Growing Up in Georgia

Khvicha Kvaratskhelia was born in Tbilisi, the capital of Georgia, into a family with strong ties to football. His father, who played for various clubs in Georgia, encouraged his passion for the sport from a young age. Growing up in a country with a rich football tradition, Khvicha showed immense potential even as a child. He played for local youth teams before joining Dinamo Tbilisi's youth academy, where he was soon recognized for his exceptional skills. His time at Dinamo Tbilisi laid the foundation for what would become an extraordinary professional career.

In addition to his football talent, Khvicha's dedication to the game and his humble attitude have made him a role model for aspiring footballers in Georgia. He has often spoken about the importance of hard work, discipline, and the support of his family in helping him reach the highest level of football.
